Charge Density 

Charge density is an amount of electric charge per unit of length, surface area or volume.

For a small change in length, dldldl and a small change in charge dqdqdq, the linear charge density, λ\lambdaλ, is equal to:

λ=dqdl.\lambda = \frac{dq}{dl}.λ=dldq​.

For a small change in surface area, dSdSdS and a small change in charge dqdqdq, the surface charge density, σ\sigmaσ, is equal to:

σ=dSdl.\sigma = \frac{dS}{dl}.σ=dldS​.

For a small change in volume, dVdVdV and a small change in charge dqdqdq, the volume charge density, ρ\rhoρ, is equal to:

ρ=dVdl.\rho = \frac{dV}{dl}.ρ=dldV​.
