Notation 

Arbitrary vectors are written in bold undercase letters (e.g. a,v\boldsymbol{a}, \boldsymbol{v}a,v). Unless stated otherwise, magnitudes of vectors are written as normal undercase letters (e.g. a,va, va,v). Physical quantities, like force (F\boldsymbol{F}F) are used by standard conventions. Matricies are written in bold uppercase letters (e.g. R\boldsymbol{R}R). Points are written in normal uppercase letters (e.g. PPP).
